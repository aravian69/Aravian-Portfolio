# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative directors, art directors, brands, and marketing agencies scouting freelance talent — they open the site once, scan the work, and decide in under a minute whether to reach out. Also general public who come through social referrals: they expect something personal and distinctive, not a resumé with thumbnails.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Abdul Aziz — VFX artist, motion designer, and AI video creator based in Jakarta. The site exists to show the quality and range of his work and convert visitors into project inquiries. Success means a visitor leaves remembering the name and knowing exactly what he does.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, artsy, bold. The work is the hero; the interface is the dark theatre around it. Confidence comes from restraint, not maximalism. Every design choice should feel like it was made by an artist who also knows code, not by a developer who added a theme.

## Anti-references

- Generic agency-template portfolios (high-contrast hero with scrolling case-study grid)
- Behance / Adobe Portfolio defaults (clean-white, photo-caption layout)
- Flashy motion-portfolio clichés: particle backgrounds, neon glow, oversaturated gradients, infinite looping text banners
- DeviantArt / ArtStation energy (dark sidebar + thumbnail grid with no editorial point of view)

## Design Principles

1. **The work speaks first.** Interface chrome is a frame, not a gallery wall. Every decorative element must earn its place by serving the work.
2. **Personal, not corporate.** This is Abdul Aziz's voice — idiosyncratic choices are features, not bugs. Avoid anything that could belong to an agency's template library.
3. **Cinematic restraint.** Drama through negative space, timing, and contrast — not through quantity of effects. One thing at a time, done with intention.
4. **Consistency as craft.** Spacing, type scale, color usage, and motion all follow a clear system. Inconsistency reads as sloppiness, not personality.
5. **Show range without noise.** Motion, VFX, AI, color — four disciplines in one grid. The navigation and filtering should make range feel like depth, not scatter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`. Custom cursor disabled on touch/hover-none devices. Dark and light themes both fully usable.
